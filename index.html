About Dani

TL;DR

I specialize in frontend work and prefer working with ReactJS and NextJS.

More About Me

I used to be a stud-welder in oil refineries across North America including Texas, Louisiana, Washington, and Canada. I loved the money but hated the work, and I dreamed of being able to use my mind and not my body.

I tried a SaaS managerial role, starting up an ecommerce business, and handling the marketing for the family tile installation business. But it was designing and developing WYSIWYG sites that got me interested in web.

Fortunately, my mother-in-law convinced me to attend a bootcamp where a passion for JavaScript was born.

In 2019, I took the first role offered to me at a boutique marketing firm in Midtown Manhattan where I built custom WordPress themes.

In 2020, I was hired at another marketing firm to maintain dozens of hardcoded PHP sites and dozens more that used DatoCMS.

In 2021, I was given the opportunity to hone my ReactJS skills by building a greenfield project for TimeTap.

This evolution as a developer has confirmed my certainty that JavaScript is my language of choice and so I am constantly looking for ways to expand and improve that skillset.
